Government of India Health Schemes

  • Ayushman Bharat - PMJAY: Provides health insurance coverage to economically disadvantaged families.
  • National Health Mission (NHM): Strengthening public healthcare services.
  • Employee State Insurance Scheme (ESIC): Health benefits for employees.
  • Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana: Maternity benefit program for pregnant women.
  • Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana: Health insurance for below-poverty-line families.
