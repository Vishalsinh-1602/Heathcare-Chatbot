Understanding Mental Health

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and behave in daily life. Seeking help when needed leads to better emotional and physical well-being.

FAQs

1. What is mental health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

2. How can I improve my mental health?

Engage in physical activity, practice mindfulness, talk to loved ones, and seek professional support if needed.

3. Where can I seek help?

There are many online and offline mental health resources, including helplines, therapists, and support groups.

Mental Health Relief Suggestions

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation
  • Engage in regular physical exercise
  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Connect with supportive friends and family
  • Seek professional counseling when needed
  • Reduce screen time and practice digital detox
